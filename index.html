Make Your Balvenie Better

There are many ways to enjoy The Balvenie. Neat, over ice or with a few drops of water are the most common ways to explore the flavours and character of our whisky in their purest form. For those seeking other ways to enjoy Balvenie, why not try a glass of whisky paired with a recommended tea, in one of our carefully designed whisky and tea cocktails. Tea and whisky share a synergy rooted in provenance and centuries of craftsmanship â€“ both require years of dedication from their master blenders, honing their skills to ensure consistency and quality in every batch created.
